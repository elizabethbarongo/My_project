What is AkiraChix

AkiraChix is a not-for-profit organization founded in April 2010 that aims to inspire and develop a successful force of women in technology who will change Africa's future.

What is AkiraChix's mission?

The mission of AkiraChix is to provide the most promising young women in Africa with technology skills to compete economically and bridge the gender gap in tech.

Who does AkiraChix serve?

AkiraChix programs are developed to reach young women at different levels, including those in primary school, high school, university, those working in technology, and those who wish to have a career in technology.

What are AkiraChix's core values?

AkiraChix's core values include:

  • Empathy
  • Diversity
  • Community
  • Life long learning
What are AkiraChix Plans for the Future?

Over the next ten years, AkiraChix aims to become the global leading organization for African women innovators in technology by:

  • Building a generation of trailblazing African women in technology
  • Strengthening financial sustainability
  • Investing in targeted research
  • Building sisterhood through alumni support
  • Scaling collaborative partnerships
